MAIN IDEA

Agricorp is a startup focused on helping individuals with limited plant care knowledge manage their home gardens. They aim to provide an innovative web application that assists users in organizing and caring for their plants by offering reminders and plant management tools.

MISSION

We aim to provide a practical and engaging way for individuals to manage the care of their plants, seamlessly integrating this process into their daily routines.

VISION

We aspire to become a leading force in environmental care for small to medium-scale settings across the nation, offering affordable solutions within the next two years.
